# ULTIMATE Immich Guide - DITCH Google/Amazon Photos

Setting up a self-hosted photo server may feel daunting at first, but with Docker and a clear step-by-step guide, it's surprisingly approachable. The effort pays off in ownership, privacy, and the satisfaction of a system that truly belongs to you. Happy photo-hoarding!

## Article Content

You now have a full-featured, self-hosted media hub that:

- **Owns your data**: All files and metadata stay on your own hardware.
- **Runs locally**: No monthly fees, no vendor lock-in.
- **Scales**: With Docker you can add more machines (e.g., an NVIDIA GPU for ML).
- **Is secure**: NetBird or any VPN keeps your traffic encrypted.

If you want to dive deeper into the following topics:

* **External libraries**: Automatically ingest media from multiple sources.
* **Fine-tuned ML**: Switch between different models or add custom training data.
* **Advanced video transcoding**: Tweaking ffmpeg settings for 4K or HEVC output.

Keep an eye on Immich's documentation and GitHub for updates. The community is very active, and contributions are always welcome.
